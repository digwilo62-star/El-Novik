The Story

A musician’s ear.
An engineer’s standard.

EL-NOVIK was built by a Nigerian musician who taught himself to play before he was tall enough to hold a full-sized guitar — then trained formally in Nigeria and the United Kingdom to learn what world-class sound actually demands.

What started as a love for the talking drum became a fluency across traditional and modern instruments, and eventually a workshop where instruments and electronics are chosen, built, and tested by someone who plays them himself. That’s the difference.
