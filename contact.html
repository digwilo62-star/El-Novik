Before You Ask

Common questions,
answered upfront.

Do you ship across Nigeria?

Yes. We deliver to every state in Nigeria. Delivery cost depends on the item’s size, weight, and your location. For large equipment like sound systems and full studio kits, we offer hand-delivery and setup within Lagos and surrounding states.

Can I see an item before I buy?

Absolutely. Visits are by appointment so we can give you proper time. Message us on WhatsApp or send a message through the form above to arrange a time. We’ll share the address once an appointment is confirmed.

What if I don’t know what I need?

That’s a normal place to start. Tell us the situation — the venue, the use case (church service, studio, school, performance), and your rough budget. We’ll recommend what actually fits, not just what’s most expensive.

Do you offer installation and setup?

Yes — for sound systems, studios, and complex audio setups. We’ll handle installation, configuration, and walk your team through how to use the equipment properly. See our services page for details.

Do you take returns?

All items are inspected before they leave us, so genuine defects are rare. If something arrives faulty, contact us within 7 days and we’ll repair, replace, or refund. For change-of-mind returns, talk to us — we’re reasonable, but custom orders and used items can’t always be returned.
