What We Build

Beyond the product.
The full sound.

Selling gear is the easy part. Making it actually sound right in your church, studio, or stage — that’s the work. Here’s how we help.

01

Sound System Design & Installation

For churches, halls, schools

We design and install full sound systems for venues that need to be heard clearly — from small chapels to multi-hundred-seat auditoriums. We handle the speaker layout, amplifier matching, mixer selection, wiring, and the acoustic adjustments that turn loud noise into clear sound.

  • Site visit and acoustic assessment
  • Equipment recommendation within your budget
  • Professional installation and wiring
  • Training for your team on day-to-day operation
Discuss a project
02

Studio Setup & Configuration

For producers, artists, content creators

Building a recording space — whether it’s a bedroom corner or a proper studio room — means more than buying a mic and an interface. We help you choose equipment that fits the room, set it up correctly, and treat the acoustics so what you record sounds like what you intended.

  • Room assessment and acoustic treatment guidance
  • Microphone, interface, and monitor selection
  • DAW and software configuration
  • Signal flow setup so everything just works
Plan your studio
03

Live Sound & Event Production

For concerts, conferences, services

Some events deserve sound that doesn’t fight the room. We provide live mixing, equipment rental, and on-site engineering for performances, conferences, and church programmes — with the kind of attention that catches problems before the audience does.

  • Equipment rental for one-off events
  • On-site sound engineering and mixing
  • Mic setup and stage monitoring
  • Soundcheck and event-day operation
Book for an event
04

Instrument & Equipment Repair

For musicians, schools, churches

Good gear doesn’t deserve to die from one bad cable or a worn part. We service and repair instruments, amplifiers, mixers, and audio electronics — including imported equipment that local technicians often won’t touch. If it’s broken, we’ll diagnose it honestly: fix it, replace a part, or tell you it’s time to let it go.

  • Instrument servicing and setup
  • Amplifier and mixer repair
  • Cable, jack, and connector replacement
  • Diagnostic-first pricing — no surprise costs
Send something for repair
05

Training & Consultation

For sound teams, worship leaders, beginners

Equipment without operators is silent equipment. We train sound teams, worship leaders, and aspiring engineers on how to actually run a mixer, EQ a vocal, manage feedback, and avoid the mistakes that ruin services and shows. Workshops can be one-off sessions or ongoing.

  • Sound desk fundamentals for worship and live use
  • Recording and producing on a Nigerian budget
  • Equipment buying advice for individuals and teams
  • On-site coaching during real services or sessions

The Process

Four steps,
no surprises.

We work the same way every time — whether the job is a single repair or a full church installation.

  1. 01

    Conversation

    You tell us what you’re building — the venue, the use case, the budget, the timeline. We listen first. No pitch, no upsell.

  2. 02

    Assessment

    We visit the space when needed, or review what you have. For installations, this is where the real engineering work begins — what equipment fits, what the room needs, what to avoid.

  3. 03

    Proposal

    You get a clear, itemised plan — what we recommend, what it costs, what it doesn’t include, and why. You decide. We don’t pressure.

  4. 04

    Build & Handover

    We do the work, we test it properly, and we walk your team through how to use it. We’re reachable after the handover — not just before it.
